About Us

Mashro3 Yanni was established by a team of professionals and amateurs, under the leadership of the Singer and Maestro / Abanoub Yanni. We present various styles and forms of songs from different times, with new harmonic and polyphonic melodic arrangements, in addition to producing songs created by us as a team, which it all aims to elevate the cultural taste, and open up to new musical ideas through the diversity of musical and artistic content.

Who is Yanni

Conductor - Composer

Abanoub Yanni.. He obtained a Bachelor of Music Education with honors.. He started learning at the age of 10, and began teaching him to play the instrument at the age of 16. He was imitated in the East by Professor Dr. (Mohamed Ashraf), Professor of Music Education in Helwan, then trained in the West with Professor (Gabriela Buda) in Debrecen, Hungary, then studied conducting the choir with the opera singer and maestro (Yannis Ben Abdullah), then he developed his musical skills by studying musical composition at the Conservatory Music Institute through listening lectures.

Abanoub Yanni is an Egyptian composer who has won several awards and certificates of appreciation for composing music for a large number of plays and feature films, most notably: "Wahm Kotar", "Lavender", and most recently "Terkat Jawhar" in 2023. Abanoub Yanni is also the founder of Doo Studio for recording, filming, and content creation in general. He is interested in studying psychology, cognitive behavioral therapy, behavior modification, and activities through his work within the Helwan Mental Health Hospital to integrate psychotherapy with art. Abanoub Yanni has taught a very large number of students in various fields and workshops in the artistic and psychological fields inside and outside Egypt.
